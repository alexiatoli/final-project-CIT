About

Applied Computing at Purdue focuses on the practical application of computer science principles to solve real-world problems. Our program equips students with in-demand technical skills in software development, networking, cybersecurity, and data management. Whether you're aiming to innovate in tech industries or improve systems in healthcare, finance, or education, Applied Computing provides a strong foundation in technology, hands-on experience, and career-ready competencies.
